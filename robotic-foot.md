## Problem
Legged robots struggle on mixed terrains due to fixed foot design.

## Solution
A terrain-adaptive system that switches soles using acoustic classification.

## My Contribution
- Developed acoustic classification pipeline
- Feature extraction + Random Forest (~93%)
- Integrated sensing with control logic
- Mechanical integration

## Results
- Improved speed
- Better efficiency across terrains

## Takeaway
Integration of mechanical design + ML + control system.
